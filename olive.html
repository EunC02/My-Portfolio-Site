About the Project

Olive is a mental health app designed to provide young adults with anonymous, accessible, and affordable support. It uses AI and verified professional guidance to help users manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health challenges in a safe and calming environment.

Process

The project started with in-depth research into user needs and mental health pain points. I created personas, mapped user journeys, and conducted usability testing to refine features such as mood tracking, therapist listings, meditation exercises, and AI chat support. Iterations focused on emotional safety, anonymity, and ease of use.
